Minimalist Design Principles

By John Doe | March 15, 2025

Minimalism in design emphasizes simplicity, clarity, and focus. This article explores core principles and practical applications.

1. Less is More

Eliminating unnecessary elements helps focus the user's attention on what truly matters.

2. Focus on Functionality

Every design element should have a clear purpose, ensuring a seamless user experience.

3. Use of White Space

Generous spacing improves readability and creates a sense of elegance.
